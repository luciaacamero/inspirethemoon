about the project

hear me out is a central florida community with the mission of amplifying the voices of small artists and businesses around them. their goal is to provide spaces in which everyone can come to be themselves and show off their talents. it started off as a “what if?” then quickly turned into a whole project with too many ideas to start with, including drinking games, clothing, events, a social media presence, and so much more.
