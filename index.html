what is inspire the moon?

inspire the moon is me, lucia. not just my work or portfolio, but myself, essentially. some call it my blood, sweat, and tears, i prefer to call it my deepest self, who i am under all other definitions of myself and what i don’t show at first.

what does it mean?

ever since i was raised with the question “what is your purpose?” i’ve had one single answer, and that is to inspire people. i’ve always felt that my goal in this world was to inspire as many people as possible the way other things inspire me to do and be better. turns out the thing that inspires me the most is the moon, even though i’m more like the sun.

my goal in life is to inspire and bring out the best in people through my art and design, because there is nothing else i imagine myself doing for the rest of my life.
